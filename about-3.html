Our Business Model and Methodologies caters to the needs and financial limitations of the SMB sector (Small and Medium Business).

Well-defined and flexible, our Business Model is a result of our deep industry intelligence and vast experience.

Nanvi's engagement models are uniquely combined with the necessities required to craft all kind of projects. Clients can choose from various Engagement Models below or a mix of these models.

Fixed Price

Nanvi offers customers a fixed cost model when the scope and specifications of the project are reasonably clear. Based on a specification prepared mentioning the cost to ensure the budget for the total project and deliverables and timelines to ensure all needs are met within stipulated timeline.

Time and Material

When scope, specification and implementation plans of a software development project are not easy to define at the outset, Time & Material Model becomes an attractive option. Through the Time & Material engagement model Nanvi gives customers greater flexibility to alter the project specification.

Milestone Billing

This model can be applied to large long-lasting projects with measurable phases. Such projects often include a series of milestones that mark the completion of different stages of the work. Payment schedules are defined on time or milestone basis. This model reinforces progress monitoring and reduces large projects risks.

Dedicated Development Team

This model will work based on the need for a dedicated pool of IT resources as per the requirement, preference and expectation.
