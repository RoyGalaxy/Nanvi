Software-Based Products & Services

You've got the idea. Nanvi can make it happen.

Nanvi's product development service gives clients the benefit of 'on-demand' technical expertise to help them build products they can market and distribute themselves. We can offer a wealth of experience in the design and development of standalone or shrink wrap applications and on-line products – and we can combine on-line and off-line technologies to deliver the best of both worlds.

Mass-Market Application Development - Special Requirements

Creating software programs that will be installed and used by thousands - or millions - of people brings special requirements:

The product often needs to be designed to work on multiple versions of Windows – sometimes it must work on Linux and Mac too. Security is a big consideration – most major companies now impose strict rules on how software can be installed, and the software installation program must work reliably in all cases. Upgrades and updates must be planned for – the program may need a special “check for updates” function. Testing of the software demands a lot of attention to ensure reliability. Often, the company behind the product needs to consider how to charge their end customers. Frequently, an on-line store and invoicing system is required. Copy protection should be considered.

On-line Product Development

In recent years, there’s been an obvious shift away from standalone or shrink-wrap applications – more and more, people use the web to do tasks previously done on their PC. There are a good number of reasons for this trend: Eliminating installed software reduces support costs. On-line systems generally don’t have a piracy problem – something that has dogged the shrink-wrap market for years. Marketing to customers of on-line systems is much easier – with download-able products, it’s much harder to connect sales back to marketing activity.
